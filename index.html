About us
Fermented food is food that has undergone a natural process of fermentation, in which microorganisms like yeast and bacteria convert carbohydrates into alcohol or acids. The alcohol or acids act as natural preservatives and create distinctive flavors, textures and aromas for fermented food.

At BGK 55555, we are passionate about bringing you the best fermented foods from around the world. Fermented foods are foods that have been transformed by natural processes of fermentation, where beneficial microbes break down sugars and produce probiotics. Probiotics are friendly bacteria that support your digestive health and immune system. Some of the fermented foods we offer are kimchi, yogurt, kefir, sauerkraut, miso, and tempeh. These foods are not only delicious, but also nutritious and good for your well-being. We invite you to explore our website and learn more about our products and services.
